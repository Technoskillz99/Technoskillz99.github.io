# Welcome to Beyond the Pitch
Hi! I'm Tomiwa. I'm in Year 10 and I'm interested in Football and Computer Science. So, I decided to use Machine Learning to solve some of the mysteries of Football.
I want to use algorithms and statistics to literally explain football with numbers. For example, by using stats that you can't find anywhere else, I can find out who 
the most underrated player on a team is.

So, why didn't I just learn privately? 
Well, I decided to make this blog as a trackpoint of my journey towards my goal. I would be using it for things like checking boxes and seeing how much progress I've made.
I also made it to document some of the processes so that one day maybe someone else might look at my code or blog and get inspired to do their own project.

Why Football?
I'm really interested in the unsung heroes of the pitch, for example, the midfielders who spread passes short and long, run the most kilometres in the whole game just for the striker to be on the frontcover of every newspaper for scoring a tapin created by a midfielder(no shade to strikers). Watching football for years has made me realise 
that there's plenty of tiny decisions happening on the pitch and I want to know if data can explain them.

So, what am I doing?
Over the next months/years I'm going to be learning new maths concepts, extending my knowledge in python and data handling, and learn machine learning which I want to pursue as a 
career while building projects that analyse football.

Final points
